  Executive Summary

         4 Credit Cards used to offset expenses for 17 nights in Europe

Chase Sapphire Preferred: Rewards used for Airfare

  • Earned 152K out of 150K-160K miles needed for Airfare
  • Needed to spend $4K in three months
  • Annual Fee is $95
  • Airfare is normally $1,200 per person during the summer months
  • This saves over $2305 [($1,200 x 2)-$95]

American Express Hilton Surpass & American Express Platinum: Rewards used for hotels

  • Earned 740,400 points and 1 free night
  • Free Hotel for 5 nights with breakfast in London (around $300 per night)
  • Free Hotel for 5 nights with breakfast in Berlin (around $200 per night)
  • Free Hotel for 5 nights with breakfast in Munich (around $250 per night)
  • Free Hotel with breakfast in Paris/Amsterdam/Brussels (around $250 per night)
  • Annual Fee $150+$695
  • This saves over $3,155 [(5 x 300) + (5 x 200) + (5 x 250) + 250 - 150 - 695]

Capital One Venture X: Rewards used for offsetting sightseeing and 1 night hotel

  • Earned 83,000 miles (equal to $830)
  • Needed to spend $4K in 3 months
  • Credited for $830 in travel expenses
  • Global entry or TSA Pre-check Reimbursement ($100)
  • Priority Pass Airport Lounge Passes ($50)
  • $250 Travel Credit Used for one night in Zurich
  • Annual Fee $95
  • This saves over $1,135 (830 + 100 + 50 + 250 - 95)

Total saved by using rewards: Almost $6,595

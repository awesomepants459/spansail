  Sample Itinerary

This itinerary is just one of many possibilities using our methods described on the home page. You can use those methods to save money in a lot of different scenarios.

This trip will give you five nights in London, and one night in your choice of Paris, Brussels, or Amsterdam. It will then take you to Berlin and Munich after that, spending five nights in each. You finish the trip in Zurich, where you will spend one night then fly out.
